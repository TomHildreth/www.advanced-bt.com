WALLGUARD PERFORMANCE DATA
  • FIRE: WallGuard or RoofGuard, formulated with 3M ceramic coating (RoofGuard has twice the amount ceramic for solar protection as WallGuard), which is sprayed, rolled, or brushed onto the exterior walls and roof at a 6-mil thickness. During Intertek's ASTM'S E2768 30-minute fire test WallGuard eared a Class-A Fire Rating with Zero flame and smoke spread. Plus it withstood 1,675℉ during Intertek's NFPA 285 2-story 30-minute fire test.
  • MOISTURE: 4 Year Wind-Driven Rain and Snow Test. The membrane is permeable (breathable) allowing interior water vapor to escape, but it is an exterior moisture barrier membrane against rain and snow. It's also resistant to fungus, mold, and mildew.
  • ELASTOMERIC
  • Tested to 600%+ elongation at 77 F. and returns to its original dimensions without deformation.
  • SOLAR
  • Passed accelerated weathering and solar exposure of 500 hours exposure (equivalent to 4 years) without degradation or signs of checking, cracking, chalking, or coating loss.
  • INSULATION
  • WallGuard and RoofGuard adds R-4.8 to the structure. Our Protected Insulation Cladding System (PICS) and it's FCB panel substrate with 1-½ of foam insulation adds R-7.7 for a total wall insulation value of R-12.5. This reduces the cost of air-conditioning by > 20%.
  • ANNUAL MAINTENANCE: None Required
  • Damaged walls can be easily repaired with WallGuard applied with paintbrush or roller. Dust buildup can be rinsed oﬀ with a garden hose.
  • REPAIR CRACKED STUCCO
  • Apply colorized WallGuard with a brush, roller or sprayer to coat stucco for a long-lasting, elastomeric (stretchy) membrane, that resists fading and water, and fills in cracks up to 1/16 wide. For wider cracks or older, chalking stucco, pre-wash, dry, pre-fill cracks with caulk, then apply WallGuard.
